Department of Computer Science

Welcome

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University offers a dynamic environment for learning, research, and innovation in computing. With a focus on interdisciplinary collaboration and cutting-edge technology, the department provides students with a comprehensive education in computer science theory, software development, and emerging technologies. Faculty members are engaged in diverse research areas including artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering. The department fosters a supportive community that prepares students for successful careers in academia, industry, and entrepreneurship.
